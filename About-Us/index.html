About the project

The Philippine University Tracker was created as a group project for our ITIPTECH subject. Our goal was to build a simple tool that helps students find and compare colleges across the country. We used API technology to gather data, ensuring that the information we provide is organized and easy to navigate.

As a team of aspiring IT professionals, this project allowed us to apply what we learned in class to a real-world problem. We are proud to share this tracker as a way to support students in their journey toward higher education.

Why Choose Us?

We built this tracker to make finding a university in the Philippines simple and organized. By using modern API technology, our team of IT students provides a fast, all-in-one tool to help you find the right school with just a few clicks.

Meet the Creators

We are a group of friends from BSIT 2B, Group 6, working together to build this Philippine University Tracker for our ITIPTECH subject. As part of our ITIPTECH subject, we teamed up to create a helpful tool for our fellow students while gaining hands-on experience with API technology.
